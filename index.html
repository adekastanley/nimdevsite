Problems

Lack of Doctors (Brain Drain)

Doctor to patients/client ratio in Nigeria 1/5000, By the year 2030, Nigeria will need a minimum of 700,000 Doctors per projected population of 300 million.

Rising Medical cost.

(out-of-pocket payments) driving Individuals and governments into bankruptcy.​ Based on current growth trend of medical expenses, government finances will not be able to support some medical expenses, and by 2035, most of the world's national medical insurance fund projects will llikely face bankruptcy.

Opacity and underutilization of medical data

Centralization could account for over 50% WASTE of medical resources, decentralised systems could harness and mitigate waste.

Solutions

Telehealth / CDSS (AI)

Augmented with an Evidence-based Clinical decisions support system, diagnostic and prescription errors are a thing of the past.​ Schedule hospital appointments, Chat with a Doctor and get that second opinion at a minimal cost. Drug Compliance reminder and remote vital signs monitoring.

Remittance.

Alternative financing value-based care in a decentralized healthcare marketplace. A perfect digital economy for the 21st century. ​ In-app finance ​

Our Decentralized Cryptocurrency Wallet" with a fully functional peer-to-peer trading facility to buy, sell, Swap, hold, and stake, crypto .make it easier for users to get quality health care services conveniently using digital assets ​ Users have FULL control of their wallets.

Digital Therapeutics 

Order Refils for OTC and get Prescriptions for ​ Medications from our Physicians guided by AI. ​ Pay with our native tokens / other digital assets for services. ​

NiSO Box
